This thread focuses on **what's actually being done** in analog gravity and gravitational physics, not science fiction. The key questions: Can laboratory systems mimic black hole physics? What have we learned? Does holographic duality connect QGP to black holes in a practical sense?

## Part 1: Analog Gravity Systems

### The Concept

**Unruh (1981):** Proposed that flowing fluids with a sonic horizon (where flow velocity exceeds sound speed) are mathematically analogous to black holes.

**The analogy:**
- Speed of sound → speed of light
- Sonic horizon → event horizon
- Phonons cannot escape upstream → photons cannot escape black hole
- Hawking radiation should exist as thermal phonon emission

### Types of Analog Systems

| System | Horizon Type | Advantage | Status |
|--------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Flowing BEC | Acoustic | Ultra-low temperature, clean | Hawking observed (2016, 2019) |
| Water waves | Surface wave | Room temperature | Limited by dispersion |
| Light in nonlinear media | Optical | Fast timescales | Stimulated Hawking (2024) |
| Weyl semimetals | Solid-state | Scalable | Theoretical/nascent experiments |
| Superfluid He-3 | Acoustic | Very clean | Difficult to control |

### Acoustic Black Holes in BEC (Steinhauer Group)

**Setup:**
- Rubidium-87 BEC at ~100 nK
- Create flow by laser manipulation
- Flow accelerates through "sonic horizon"

**Key results:**

**2014:** First observation of self-amplifying Hawking radiation
**2016:** Quantum entanglement between Hawking partners observed
- Phonon outside horizon entangled with partner inside
- Confirms quantum nature of process

**2019:** Thermal spectrum confirmed
- Hawking radiation has Planckian spectrum
- Temperature determined by "surface gravity" at horizon
- **Validates Hawking's 1974 prediction**

**What this proves:**
- Hawking radiation is a generic feature of horizons (not specific to gravity)
- Quantum vacuum fluctuations at horizon split into real particles
- The mechanism is robust across different physics

### Optical Analogs

**Concept:**
- Intense light pulse in nonlinear medium creates refractive index gradient
- Light trailing behind pulse experiences "event horizon"
- Photons cannot catch up → trapped

**Experiments:**
- 2024: Stimulated Hawking radiation observed in optical fiber
- Temperature ~10³ K (high compared to astrophysical black holes!)

**Limitation:** These are STIMULATED emission, not spontaneous Hawking radiation.

## Part 2: Condensed Matter Analogs

### Weyl Semimetals as Spacetime Analogs

**What are Weyl semimetals?**
- Crystalline materials with linear band crossings (Weyl points)
- Low-energy excitations behave as massless Weyl fermions
- Band structure creates effective metric for quasiparticles

**Type-II Weyl semimetals:**
- Dirac cone is "tilted" beyond vertical
- Creates electron and hole pockets at Weyl point
- **The tilt acts like a black hole horizon**

**The analog:**
```
In GR:  ds² = -(1 - v/c)² dt² + dx²/(1 - v/c) ...
In WS:  E(k) = ℏvk + ℏuk  (tilted cone)

When u > v: "horizon" for propagating modes
```

**Heterostructure experiments:**
- Type-I / Type-II Weyl semimetal interface
- Creates artificial event horizon
- Predicts Hawking-like thermal conductance

**Current status:**
- Theoretical framework established
- Specific signatures predicted (conductance peaks)
- No definitive experimental confirmation yet

### Graphene and 2D Materials

**Tilted Dirac cones in graphene:**
- Strain can tilt the Dirac cone
- Creates effective curved spacetime
- Potentially tunable "gravity"

**Potential applications:**
- Desktop tests of Hawking radiation
- Probe quantum gravity effects at low energy
- Analog cosmology (expanding universe simulations)

## Part 3: Micro Black Hole Searches at LHC

### The Theoretical Motivation

**Extra dimension models (ADD, RS):**
- If extra spatial dimensions exist, gravity could become strong at TeV energies
- Effective Planck mass could be M_Pl* ~ 1-10 TeV (instead of 10¹⁹ GeV)
- Particle collisions at LHC might produce micro black holes

**What would happen:**
1. Two protons collide at √s = 13 TeV
2. If impact parameter < Schwarzschild radius at TeV scale → black hole forms
3. Black hole evaporates via Hawking radiation in ~10⁻²⁶ seconds
4. Produces burst of particles (high multiplicity, high temperature)

### LHC Search Results

**CMS and ATLAS searches (2011-2024):**

| Search | √s (TeV) | Result | Limit |
|--------|----------|--------|-------|
| CMS 2012 | 7 | No events | M_BH > 4.3 TeV |
| ATLAS 2013 | 8 | No events | M_BH > 5.3 TeV |
| CMS 2016 | 13 | No events | M_BH > 9.5 TeV |
| Combined | 13 | **No black holes found** | M_Pl* > 3.5 TeV (n=2) |

**Interpretation:**
- Extra dimension models with low Planck scale (< 3.5 TeV) excluded
- Standard Model (no extra dimensions) consistent with data
- TeV-scale gravity NOT observed

### What the Non-Detection Means

**For extra dimensions:**
- If extra dimensions exist, their effects are not visible at current LHC energies
- Planck scale likely >> TeV (possibly 10¹⁹ GeV as in standard physics)
- Need much higher energies to test (not achievable)

**For quantum gravity:**
- Quantum gravity effects likely only at Planck scale (~10¹⁹ GeV)
- 6 orders of magnitude beyond current collider reach
- **Cannot directly test quantum gravity with foreseeable technology**

### Future Colliders

**Proposed 100 TeV collider (FCC-hh):**
- Could probe M_Pl* ~ 10 TeV
- Still no guarantee of extra dimension effects
- Would improve limits significantly

**But fundamentally:**
True Planck-scale physics is ~10¹⁶× beyond LHC energies. Direct black hole production requires conditions not achievable in laboratory.

## Part 4: The Holographic Connection (AdS/CFT)

### What is AdS/CFT?

**Maldacena conjecture (1997):**
A theory of gravity in (d+1)-dimensional Anti-de Sitter space is EQUIVALENT to a conformal field theory in d dimensions without gravity.

**In simpler terms:**
- Black hole in 5D AdS space ↔ thermal plasma in 4D boundary
- Gravity in bulk ↔ strongly coupled gauge theory on boundary
- **A dictionary relates quantities in both descriptions**

### The QGP-Black Hole Connection

**Key insight:**
Quark-gluon plasma at RHIC/LHC is a STRONGLY coupled fluid. Perturbative QCD fails. But holographic duality provides exact results!

**The viscosity calculation:**
Using black brane (black hole extended in space directions) in AdS:

$$\frac{\eta}{s} = \frac{1}{4\pi} \approx 0.08$$

where η = shear viscosity, s = entropy density.

**This is the KSS bound** — conjectured to be the minimum for ANY fluid.

**Experimental comparison:**
- QGP measured: η/s ≈ 0.1 - 0.2 (close to bound!)
- Water: η/s ≈ 9
- Superfluid helium: η/s ≈ 0.3
- **QGP is the most perfect fluid ever observed**

### What This Means

**Is QGP really a black hole?**
NOT literally. But the mathematics describing QGP is IDENTICAL to that of a black hole in one higher dimension.

**Implications:**
1. Heavy-ion experiments probe black hole physics (in dual description)
2. Can calculate QGP properties using gravitational methods
3. Quantum gravity effects encoded in QGP transport coefficients

**This is the most concrete connection between laboratory physics and black holes.**

### Limitations of Holography

**What AdS/CFT can do:**
- Relate strongly coupled gauge theories to gravity
- Predict universal properties (like η/s bound)
- Guide intuition about non-perturbative physics

**What AdS/CFT cannot do:**
- Tell us about OUR universe (our cosmology is not AdS)
- Produce actual black holes
- Directly test Planck-scale physics

## Part 6: The Fundamental Limit

### Why We Can't Make Real Black Holes

**Schwarzschild radius for 1 kg:**
$$r_s = \frac{2GM}{c^2} = \frac{2 \times 6.67 \times 10^{-11} \times 1}{(3 \times 10^8)^2} \approx 1.5 \times 10^{-27} \text{ m}$$

This is ~10¹² times smaller than a proton!

**To make a black hole with M = 1 kg:**
- Must compress 1 kg into sphere of radius 10⁻²⁷ m
- Requires pressures ~ 10⁵⁰ Pa (impossible)

**At LHC energies (13 TeV = 2 × 10⁻²³ kg):**
$$r_s = 3 \times 10^{-50} \text{ m}$$

This is 10³⁵ times smaller than Planck length! LHC energies are NOWHERE CLOSE to forming black holes in standard physics.

### The Extra Dimension Loophole (Closed)

**If extra dimensions lowered Planck scale to TeV:**
- Black hole formation could occur at LHC
- LHC searches found NOTHING
- Loophole effectively closed (M_Pl* > 3.5 TeV)

### What Would Be Needed

**To directly probe Planck scale:**
- Particle collider with √s ~ 10¹⁹ GeV
- This is 10⁶× higher than LHC
- Energy of ~1 kg concentrated in single collision
- **Completely impossible with foreseeable technology**

## Part 7: Summary — The Actual Frontier

### What We CAN Do

| Capability | Current Status | What It Teaches |
|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Analog Hawking radiation | **Observed** (BEC 2016, 2019) | Horizon physics is universal |
| QGP as holographic dual | **Confirmed** (η/s matches) | Strongly coupled systems ~ gravity |
| Weyl semimetal horizons | **Predicted**, not yet observed | Condensed matter analog gravity |
| LHC black hole search | **Null result** | No low-scale extra dimensions |

### What We CANNOT Do

| Goal | Barrier | Gap |
|------|---------|-----|
| Produce real micro black holes | Need Planck energy | 10⁶× beyond LHC |
| Directly test quantum gravity | Same | 10⁶× |
| Access singularity physics | Requires actual black hole | Impossible |
| Solve information paradox experimentally | Need controlled black hole formation/evaporation | Impossible |

### The Leverage Assessment

**High leverage:**
- **Holographic QGP studies:** Real connection between accessible physics and gravity
- **Analog black holes:** Test universal quantum vacuum physics

**Medium leverage:**
- **Weyl semimetal experiments:** New probes of effective horizons
- **Future collider searches:** Could rule out/discover extra dimensions

**No leverage (currently):**
- **Actual black hole production:** Completely inaccessible
- **Planck-scale physics:** 10⁶× beyond reach

### Conclusion

The "artificial black hole" frontier is mostly about:
1. **Analog systems** that reproduce some features of horizon physics
2. **Holographic duality** that connects QGP to black branes mathematically
3. **Null searches** that constrain exotic physics (extra dimensions)

None of this produces actual black holes or accesses true Planck-scale quantum gravity. The fundamental barrier — concentrating energy to Schwarzschild scale — remains insurmountable.

**However:** Analog experiments have confirmed that Hawking radiation IS REAL. This is a profound result, validating one of the most important predictions connecting quantum mechanics and general relativity.
